Game of Thrones is a fantasy drama TV series. It's adapted 
from A Song of Ice and Fire, a series of fantasy novels by 
George R. R. Martin. The story is set in Westeros with 
multiple story arcs.

The main story focuses on the the power struggle in claiming 
the Iron Throne. It's basically set in medieval times, but 
with dragons, magic, and undead creatures. The story starts 
out grounded in a more reality based world. However, we find 
out magical creatures lurk in the icy shadows.

As time progresses, the war of the Irone Throne tranforms 
into different stories, of struggle, freedom, and conquest.

Multiple stories converge into 1 main story arc, by the end.
Then the story flames out and ends.
